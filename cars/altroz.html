Technical Specifications

The Gold Standard of hatchbacks, offering power and efficiency across three engine types.

Powertrains

  • 1.2L Revotron Petrol: 88 PS | 115 Nm
  • 1.2L iCNG: 73.5 PS | 103 Nm
  • 1.5L Revotorq Diesel: 90 PS | 200 Nm
  • Transmission: 5MT / 5AMT / 6DCA

Dimensions & Capacity

  • L x W x H: 3990 x 1755 x 1523 mm
  • Ground Clearance: 165 mm
  • Boot Space: 345L (Petrol/Diesel) | 210L (CNG)
  • Fuel Tank: 37L (Petrol/Diesel)

Chassis & Wheels

  • Brakes: Front Disc | Rear Drum
  • Tyre Size: 185/60 R16
  • Suspension: Independent Front / Semi-Independent Rear
  • Safety: 6 Airbags (Standard) | 5-Star GNCAP

Premium Indulgence

The Altroz interior is a masterclass in craftsmanship. Featuring ambient mood lighting, a wearable key for entry, and a flat-bottom steering wheel for a sporty feel.

Laser-Cut Design

The Altroz stands out with its sharp, laser-cut lines and a sleek profile. Its ALFA architecture ensures that style never comes at the cost of safety or performance.
